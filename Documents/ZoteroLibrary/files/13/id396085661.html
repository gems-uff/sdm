Description

Manage your own game company and try to create a million-selling game in this unique simulation.

Features the ability to develop your company's own game console, plus a system for changing your staff members' professions.

Hire talented people and train them to develop their skills.

As your staff gets more experience, you will unlock a wider array of game genres and content to develop. Try to find the most popular combinations and develop for the latest platform!
Your staff members can have a variety of game-related professions, from programmer to sound engineer.

Work hard and you may reach the top of the video game industry!

Universal app supports iPhone, iPod touch, and iPad. Fully touch screen interface.

Customer Reviews

Needs MORE, MORE, AND MORE!
     
by Apple and Nintendo Fan #1

*If you want to know how to get a hardware engineer than click more on this review and on the bottom it will say how*

Game Dev Story is a great game, I have been addicted for many hours, and even when I beat the game I just come back for more. It is one of those games that you will play it again and again with no hesitation. HOWEVER, it needs a lot more. In my opinion, it kind of makes me feel like your company (in the game) is in the shadows and when you release a game and someone is at a game store and sees your games then they'd say "I never knew Sunshine Inc. (just an example company) released a game." It needs the stuff I listed below:
• The ability to tell your fans about your company releasing a game (other than advertising, like with an interview, a trailer video, exc.)
• When you release a console then other companies can develop games for your console
• The ability to test try your game for a minute before you release it, so you can decide if you will release it or throw it away
• In Gamedex, you can make a list of games that you are going to release in the future, and if you're working on a console, then your fans can test it out
• Design how your console looks and functions, it shouldn't always look the same and function the same
• Design the video game cover
• Have a website for your company, and you can do something kind of like what Nintendo does, you can have an online shop for your company and an online reward club for your company. You can a hire more employees in another building to ship online orders and rewards
• Your game name can be longer than 14 characters

GET RID OF THIS:
• Blackouts. I might have 257 fun on my game, then a blackout occurs and I would have a lot less, for example, after that blackout I would have 112 fun. You should do what another reviewer said, at every percent your company saves the game in progress on a CD or disk

The game is so fun I am not dropping it down even 1 star for lacking some things. Kairosoft, I know I'm asking for a lot but please do all of this.

*HOW TO GET A HARDWARE ENGINEER: You have to level up one of your employees to level 5 on all of the available jobs (sound engineer, designer, producer, exc.). You need the job switching papers from the salesman that comes on every 1st week of May. After you level that person up to level 5 on all jobs, you need to use another job switching paper to switch the person's job to Hardware Engineer. After that you can start creating consoles. Hope it helps. 
